Nasi Goreng

Description

Fried rice is a delicious food and is traditionally made from rice fried in a wok or frying pan. Fried rice can be made with a variety of ingredients, including all types of vegetables, meat, and eggs. Not only is it easy to make, but it also tastes really delicious. If you want to know how to make fried rice, follow these steps.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups white rice
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 medium size onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 3 eggs
  • a little black pepper
  • teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • Chives to decorate
  • 1/4 kg cooked chicken meat

Steps

  1. Cook 4 cups white rice. Put the rice into boiling water and cook until cooked. Some types of rice take about 10 minutes and other types of rice take up to 30 minutes to cook. You can also cook rice in a microwave or rice cooker, it just won't taste as good.

  2. Prepare vegetables. First, wash 2 cups of carrots, 1 onion, 1 clove of garlic, one stick of ginger, and 1 cup of bean sprouts. Then chop the carrots and onions, and grind 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger. Set these ingredients aside.

  3. Pour 2 tablespoons vegetable oil into a large frying pan. The frying pan you use must be deep enough, like a frying pan, use medium heat.

  4. Cook the vegetables in the frying pan for 3 minutes. Add the carrots, onions, garlic and ginger to the frying pan. Add 1 teaspoon of salt and a pinch of black pepper. The vegetables will soften slightly but should not turn brown.

  5. Put 1/4 kg of cooked chicken into the frying pan. You can use the chicken you cooked for another meal yesterday, or buy chicken that is already cooked, or prepare special chicken to make fried rice first. Dice the chicken thinly and add to the frying pan.

  6. Put 2 tablespoons of sesame oil into the frying pan. You can add the oil a little at a time as needed -- you don't have to add it all at once

  7. Add three eggs to the frying pan. Crack the eggs, put them in a bowl, and beat them first. Then put the beaten egg into the frying pan.

  8. Put the rice into the frying pan. Fry the rice and other ingredients for 2 - 3 minutes, until the rice is hot and the ingredients are evenly mixed. Add 3 tablespoons of soy sauce to the rice mixture and fry everything for another 30 seconds. Then, remove the frying pan from the stove.

  9. Serve. Place the rice on a plate and garnish with sliced spring onions. Enjoy this fried rice as a main meal.
